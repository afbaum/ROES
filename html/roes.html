This site is designed to help you find the exact item number for a specific hearing aid. You will be able to copy and paste the item number into ROES and order exactly what you need for the patient. No more trying to explain to your Tech the difference between Hear Clear, Cerustops, HF4 Pro, and NanoCare. This website will save you and your tech time and the VA money which is normaly lost by ordering the wrong parts.
